A CYPRESS BAY CLUB PASSIONATE ABOUT ANIMALS

We love animals and what we do.

About Us

We are a club located in Cypress Bay High School devoted to helping and caring for animals. We believe it is egregious how many animals suffer in our modern day society and are working every day to relieve the pain of animals. Our board consists of eight members, each dedicated contributors of our cause.

ANYONE IS CAPABLE OF HELPING

You don't have to be some animal activist or enthusiast in order to help out animals. If you can spare even one hour a week at a shelter or rasing donations, that would be enough to save multiple lives.

You don't have to be some animal activist or enthusiast in order to join our club. All you have to do is show up to the meetings and you are making a world of difference.

You don't have to be some animal activist or enthusiast in order to donate or participate. We accept donations from anyone and everyone as well as allow anyone to join us.
